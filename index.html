DIODE: A Dense Indoor and Outdoor DEpth Dataset

By Toyota Technologicial Institute at Chicago

DIODE (Dense Indoor and Outdoor DEpth) is a a dataset that contains diverse high resolution color images with accurate, dense, wide-range depth measurements. It is the first public dataset to include RGBD images of indoor and outdoor scenes obtained with one sensor suite. For more information, our paper is available on arXiv.

    Data Formats

    The dataset consists of RGB images, depth maps and depth masks. Their formats are as follows:

    RGB images (*.png): RGB images with the resolution of 1024 × 768.

    Depth maps (*_depth.npy): Depth ground truth with the same resolution of the images.

    Depth masks (*_depth_mask.npy): Binary depth validity masks.
